Printers are the quintessential accessories that one must possess if you have a computer at home. These peripheral instruments help you in producing a hard copy of an electronic document in no time. From personal printers at home to networked printers shared by a group at places like an office or a cyber cafe, creating a physical copy of important files or documents is now a cake walk.

With the advent of new technology over the decades, printers have undergone radical changes in the way they function. From noisy and bulky machines, these instruments have transformed into sleek devices with catchy appearance. Their functionalities too have modernised to make the user experience even more hassle-free. Moreover, these days, you can even get stuff printed directly from external storage devices like a memory card or a hard disk.

Printers come in several varieties based on the technology they employ for printing an article. While some modern-technology printers employ laser to create representations of high quality texts with lots of graphics, some devices use liquid ink for the job. You must have seen the credit card readers at malls or shops. The receipts that these machines print are generated by thermal printers on heat-sensitive papers.
